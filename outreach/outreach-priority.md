## Outreach Workflow

1. **Day 1-14:** Contact 1 Tier 1 lead per day (10 business days)
2. **Day 15-28:** Move to Tier 2 (20 leads, 1-2 per day)
3. **Day 29+:** Begin Tier 3; re-engage non-responders from earlier tiers
4. **After 7 days no response:** Send one follow-up (short, casual)
5. **After 14 days no response:** Move to cold; try alternative channel
6. **If reply received:** Log response, update status, determine next action

### Status Codes
- **✔ Sent** — Initial message sent
- **⏳ Followed up** — Follow-up sent after no reply
- **💬 Replied** — Received a response (log details)
- **❌ Declined** — Not interested; note reason
- **✅ Interested** — Buyer engaged (escalate)
- **➡️ Referred** — Lead sent a referral
