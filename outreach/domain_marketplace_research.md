# Domain Marketplace Research — Best Platforms for Listing Communiq8 Bundle

## Executive Summary
For a premium €20,000 domain bundle, we need platforms that:
1. Handle high-value transactions (€5k+) securely with escrow
2. Have active buyer traffic in the premium domain segment
3. Offer visibility to both communication/tech buyers and Middle East/Kuwait buyers
4. Provide brokerage or concierge services for large deals

---

## 1. Sedo
**Website:** sedo.com  
**Best for:** Premium domain sales, broad global reach  
**Listing fee:** Free basic listing / ~€59 for premium featured listing  
**Commission:** 10-15% (varies by sale price; ~15% for €20k = ~€3,000)  
**Pros:**
- Largest domain marketplace globally (19M+ listings)
- Strong European buyer base (good for .co.uk buyer)
- Built-in escrow and transfer services
- Broker-assisted sales available for premium listings
- Strong SEO, domains appear in search results
- Pay-per-click parking revenue while listed

**Cons:**
- High commission (10-15%)
- Competitive — thousands of premium .com listings
- Broker service has additional fees
- Middle East buyer segment is weaker than Western buyers

**Recommendation:** ✅ PRIMARY LISTING — essential for any premium domain sale

---

## 2. Afternic (GoDaddy)
**Website:** afternic.com  
**Best for:** Fast sales, GoDaddy exposure, .com dominance  
**Listing fee:** Free  
**Commission:** 15-20% (varies by partner program)  
**Pros:**
- Owned by GoDaddy — biggest domain registrar globally
- Syndicated to GoDaddy's massive buyer base (80M+ domains under management)
- Fast transfer process through GoDaddy
- Best-in-class for .com liquidity
- "Fast Transfer" option enables instant sales
- Good for .com and .net sales

**Cons:**
- High commission (15-20% for non-exclusive)
- Lower commission (10%) if listed exclusively
- GoDaddy's interface and user experience is dated
- Kuwait-specific visibility is limited

**Recommendation:** ✅ PRIMARY LISTING — especially for the .com, pair with Sedo

---

## 3. DAN.COM
**Website:** dan.com  
**Best for:** Premium domain landing pages, seller-friendly terms  
**Listing fee:** Free  
**Commission:** 9% flat (lower than Sedo/Afternic for €20k)  
**Additional:** €69/year for premium DAN landing page  
**Pros:**
- Lowest commission among major premium platforms (9%)
- Beautiful, conversion-optimised landing pages
- Built-in escrow and payment plans (buyers can pay in instalments)
- Strong mobile experience
- Good for European and global buyers
- Transparent pricing — what you see is what you get

**Cons:**
- Smaller buyer base than Sedo or Afternic
- Less syndication power
- .co.uk and .biz less common here
- Fewer Middle East buyers than Western

**Recommendation:** ✅ SECONDARY LISTING — low commission makes it a must-use

---

## 4. Flippa
**Website:** flippa.com  
**Best for:** Domain + website bundles, auction-style sales  
**Listing fee:** $19-$49 depending on listing type  
**Commission:** 10% (capped at $1,000 for domains under $50k)  
**Pros:**
- Auction format can create urgency and bidding competition
- Active community of domain investors and end-users
- Lower commission cap = cheaper than alternatives
- Good for premium .com and brandable domains
- Growing Middle East user base

**Cons:**
- More speculative/domain investor crowd, fewer genuine end-users
- Auction format may not suit fixed-price premium bundle
- Less sophisticated escrow/transfer than Sedo or DAN
- Bundle listing (4 domains) may be confusing on their platform

**Recommendation:** ⚡ OPTIONAL — use auction format if we want quick sale; list as "brand bundle"

---

## 5. Atom (formerly Brandpa / BrandBucket)
**Website:** atom.com  
**Best for:** Brandable domain names, startup buyers  
**Listing fee:** Free (or $39 for expedited review)  
**Commission:** 25-30% (on the higher side)  
**Pros:**
- Specialises in brandable, catchy domains — perfect for communiq8
- Strong startup and founder buyer base (Persona 1)
- Beautiful brand-oriented listing pages with valuations
- Curated marketplace — premium positioning
- Active social media promotion of listings

**Cons:**
- Very high commission (25-30%)
- Selectively curated — may not accept a bundle
- Focus is on brandable names, not bundle/premium plays
- Longer listing approval process
- Kuwait buyers less likely to browse here

**Recommendation:** ⚡ OPTIONAL — high commission but startup audience aligns with Persona 1

## Honourable Mentions

| Platform | Why Consider | Why Skip |
|---|---|---|
| **GoDaddy Auctions** | Massive traffic, built-in GoDaddy audience | Better to use Afternic (same owner, better structure) |
| **Namecheap Marketplace** | Low fees, growing user base | Smaller buyer pool for premium sales |
| **Efty** | Beautiful landing pages, multi-platform syndication | Requires self-managed outreach, no built-in marketplace traffic |
| **DomainAgents** | Broker-assisted, buyer makes offer | Less traffic volume |
| **Sav.com** | Modern UI, low fees | Still building premium buyer traffic |
| **Uniregistry** | Good for international sales | Reduced activity, acquired by GoDaddy |
| **Kuwait-specific platforms** | 4Sale.com.kw, OpenSooq.com | Not domain marketplaces, but could list in business section |
